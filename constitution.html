School Rules and General Constitution

The code of ethics, structural operational frameworks, and foundational regulations governing the behavior of all members within Namboole High School.

Preamble & Foundational Ethics

This constitution is established to maintain an orderly, progressive, and highly academic environment at Namboole High School. It is intended to guide students, staff, and institutional managers toward absolute self-discipline, safety, and mutual respect.

Article I: General Conduct and Discipline

Section 1.1: Respect and Obedience
Every student must exhibit absolute respect to school leadership, academic faculty, auxiliary staff, and fellow students. Insubordination or derogatory language will attract strict administrative actions.

Section 1.2: General Campus Uniform Code
Students must appear smart and wear full, specified school uniforms during standard academic hours, travel times, and public functions. Alterations to uniform styles are strictly prohibited.

Section 1.3: Absolute Punctuality
Punctual attendance is mandatory for morning assemblies, classroom lessons, prep hours, sports routines, and institutional gatherings. Latecomers will receive corrective manual labor exercises.

Article II: Prohibited Actions and Contraband

Section 2.1: Banned Electronic Gadgets
Smartphones, radios, personal music players, and independent web devices are completely illegal on campus for students, unless explicitly requested for specialized science practical sessions by faculty.

Section 2.2: Substances & Narcotics
The possession, sharing, or use of alcohol, tobacco products, or illegal intoxicating narcotics inside institutional perimeters represents a capital offense leading to immediate expulsion.

Section 2.3: Bullying and Intimidation
Namboole High School operates a zero-tolerance policy against any form of bullying, initiation rituals, teasing, or physical fighting. Perpetrators will face instant suspension pending a disciplinary committee verdict.

Disciplinary Framework

Infractions against these rules are processed transparently via the following sequence:

  1. Verbal Warning & Counseling: For primary minor rule slips.
  2. Official Written Notice: Dispatched to parents for repetitive minor errors.
  3. Disciplinary Panel Hearing: Formal defense layout facing the Board.
  4. Suspension / Expulsion: For definitive major offenses outlined under Article II.
