General Rules & Code of Discipline

Every student at Namboole High School is expected to represent the institution with integrity and high moral standing[cite: 4]. The following foundational statutes govern daily routines[cite: 4]:

  • Section 1: Academic Integrity & Punctuality All students must attend scheduled lessons, morning assemblies, and laboratory presentations on time[cite: 4]. Academic dishonesty or cheating results in immediate disciplinary review[cite: 4].
  • Section 2: Dress Code and Representation The official school uniform must be clean, un-modified, and worn smartly at all times with the explicit badge visible during regular operations and regional travel[cite: 4].
  • Section 3: Protection of Institutional Property Malicious damage to science equipment, dormitories, computers, or shared database hubs is strictly forbidden[cite: 4]. Compensation is required for any broken infrastructure components[cite: 4].
  • Section 4: Electronic Gadget Policies Unauthorized standard smart devices or entertainment systems are strictly regulated outside specific technical instruction blocks to preserve focus within class streams[cite: 4].
